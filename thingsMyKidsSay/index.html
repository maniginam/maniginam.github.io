Things My Kids Say

Right now, Rex is the only one who can talk, but I want to capture some of the things he says, because they are just so funny & nonsensical--and he has no idea!

  • Mom: Rex What do you want for dinner? Rex: Umm... Trucks!
  • Rex: {picking up a tree star} Look Mom! A Star Fish!
  • Rex: Mom, I need a donut! And I need cake.
  • {7/8/2020 at bed time} Mom: Good Night Rex. Rex: Mom! I need a transfer truck and a green flatbed truck! Mom: {obliges and meets his request} Rex: Not this truck!! A different truck!
  • {at bed time} Dad: Good Night Rex. Rex: Umm... Wait. I need a man, a cone, and a truck! Dad: {obliges and gets a man, a cone, and a truck} Rex: Not this truck!! A different truck!
