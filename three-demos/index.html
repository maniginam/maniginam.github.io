Three.js Concept Demos

Interactive previews of each idea. Hover and interact with the canvases to explore.

Concept 01

Particle Cloud Name

Your name rendered as a dense point cloud. Particles scatter on mouse hover and gently reform. Purple/coral palette on dark background.

Concept 02

3D Terrain with Traffic

Elevation terrain with roads and glowing traffic particles. Camera orbits overhead. Builds on your existing hero data — daylight drives the lighting.

Concept 03

Shader Flow Background

GLSL fragment shader with flowing noise gradients in your purple/coral palette. Lightweight, mesmerizing, great as a section background.

Concept 04

Interactive Globe

A wireframe globe with glowing project pins. Slowly auto-rotates, drag to explore. Perfect for an AI projects showcase section.

Concept 05

Low-Poly City Skyline

Procedural low-poly buildings with lit windows at night. Skyline silhouette with ambient lighting that could sync with your SunCalc system.

Concept 06

3D Floating Blog Cards

Blog posts as floating cards in 3D space, arranged on a helix. Scroll or drag to browse. A spatial archive for your writing.

Concept 07

Generative Landscape

Procedural terrain with animated noise. Infinite scrolling landscape with fog and moody lighting. Great for a /lab experiments page.

Concept 08

Geometry Morphing

Shapes morph between forms on hover — representing page transitions. Smooth vertex animation between cube, sphere, torus, and more.
